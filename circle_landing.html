How it works

Three steps to
your people

CIRCLE removes every barrier between you and a night worth remembering.

01
Tell us your vibe

Set up a quick profile — your interests, the types of experiences you enjoy, and the kind of social environment you thrive in.

02
Discover curated events

Browse a feed of handpicked experiences built around your profile — not a random algorithm. Creative workshops, social dinners, quiz nights, rooftop sessions.

03
Know before you go

See who else is attending and chat with them before the event. Arrive knowing faces, not strangers. Show up to a circle, not a crowd.
